Standard Communities' CXO priorities.

Sourced from co-founder public statements and Standard's press release on Christiana's mandate — not yet internally validated.

01 · Scale 50,000 units under management by 2030

Standard has grown to 28,000+ units and closed a $1B acquisition in 2024 — the largest affordable-housing deal of the year. Reaching 50K by 2030 means nearly doubling the portfolio in four years. Capital and deal flow are in place; the constraint is operational infrastructure that scales without proportional headcount.

02 · Infrastructure Scalable systems & processes for the next phase of growth

Christiana was hired in July 2025 to build scalable processes, strengthen the structural foundation, and align teams and systems. She is less than a year into the mandate and in active infrastructure-design mode — this is her current job, not a future initiative.

03 · Data Real-time data transparency across a distributed PMC network

Standard works with 10 property management companies, each on different systems. Co-founder Scott Alter: "If we don't have real-time data, we're essentially operating in the past." An in-house team centralizes it manually — a bottleneck that compounds as the portfolio grows.
